README 32X10_raw
-----------------
Matrix containing the responce value (EMG peak responce) in a window time
of 40 ms after the stimulation offset.
	dim 1 : number of stimulation channels
	dim 2 : x,y coordinates of the ch +10 reps responce
	dim 3 : emg channel number (4)

NB: the mapping of the implant is the following: 

     1    10    26    17
     3    12    28    19
     5    14    30    21
     7    16    32    23
     2     9    25    18
     4    11    27    20
     6    13    29    22
     8    15    31    24
